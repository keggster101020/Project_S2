Cards



There are three sizes of cards: small, medium, and large each with their own purpose. The large card is primaraly used as it can dispaly images, support title text, subtitle text and regular description text. The other two card types are displaying smaller information but in an important way.

All card types have class s-card followed by the s-card--(size) identifier.

Any aspect of a card type (images, title,subtitle, action-bar, or text) can be omitted.


Small Card

Small cards are meant for small images with a title and a subtitle only both containing minmal text and an optional action bar
